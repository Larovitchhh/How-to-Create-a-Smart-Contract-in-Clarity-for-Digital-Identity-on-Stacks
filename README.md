How to Create a Smart Contract in Clarity for Digital Identity on Stacks
Digital identity is one of the most powerful use cases of blockchain technology. On Stacks Blockchain, identities can be built in a secure, transparent, and Bitcoin-anchored way using the Clarity smart contract language.

This article walks step by step through designing, writing, and deploying a digital identity smart contract in Clarity, with practical examples and best practices.
